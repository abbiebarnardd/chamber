Laguna Beach

Laguna Beach is smaller coastal community in Southern California. It is knonw for being idylic and full of art. It is home to beautiful beaches and gorgeous views. Vistors have a broad range of activities to enjoy including spending an afternoon in Heisler Park, visting Main Beach, and attending the Sawdust Art Festival.

Newport Beach

Newport Beach is known for its sandy beaches and for being a prime swimming destination. Visitors can enjoy activities such as whale watching, visiting Crystal Cove, watching surfers at The Wedge, exploring the Newport Harbor, going shopping at some of the finest malls, and visiting Balboa Pier and Balboa Fun Zone.
