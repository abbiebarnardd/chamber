About Us

Bountiful Foods is a family owned business that was established in 2014. My name is Abbie Barnard and I am the founder. In 2014, I had severe gut malfunctions that I could not find a permanent solution for. The more doctors I visited, the more clear it bacame to me that the solutions being presented were a "bandaid" approach that were merely treating symptoms, not finding a cure. I researched heavily and made diet changes that not only healed my gut but also gave me my life back. At Bountiful Fruits, our hearts are invested in providing the produce and food necessary to allow others to find the health that will allow them to live their happiest lives. Please feel free to reach out with questions and comments.
